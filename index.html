Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How long until I see results?
Most pet parents notice improvements in digestion within 3-7 days, while behavioral benefits typically begin after 10-14 days of consistent use. For best results, we recommend using The Dog Edit™ daily for at least 30 days.
Is it safe for all dogs?
Yes! The Dog Edit™ is formulated for dogs of all sizes, breeds, and ages. The chews are free from common allergens like chicken and dairy. As with any supplement, we always recommend consulting with your veterinarian before starting a new regimen, especially for dogs with existing health conditions.
What if my dog doesn't like it?
We've formulated The Dog Edit™ to be tasty even for picky eaters. However, if your dog isn't a fan, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Just let us know within 30 days, and we'll refund your purchase — no questions asked and no need to return the product.
How many chews per day?
Dosage depends on your dog's weight: Dogs under 25 lbs: 1 chew daily; 25-50 lbs: 2 chews daily; Over 50 lbs: 3 chews daily. Each bag contains 30 chews, so depending on your dog's size, one bag will last 10-30 days.
