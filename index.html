Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What does Neem do for dogs?
Neem is a natural, plant-based immune booster traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine. It supports your dog’s defenses and helps repel pests like ticks, fleas, and worms naturally from the inside out.
Is it safe to combine all three benefits in one chew?
Yes! We worked with formulators and veterinarians to create a safe, balanced blend where each active ingredient supports a different system (gut, nervous system, immune system) without negative interactions or overload at the recommended dosage.
Can I give this daily?
Absolutely. This chew is designed to be a daily wellness ritual, not just a temporary fix. Regular, consistent use helps build gut balance, reduce anxiety, and support immune resilience over time for long-term health benefits.
Why not just use a flea/tick collar or pill?
Topical treatments and chemical pills primarily target pests externally or after they bite. The Dog Edit™ focuses on long-term wellness by supporting your dog’s *internal defenses* naturally with Neem. Think of it as strengthening their system from within. Many parents use both approaches for comprehensive protection.
How long until I see results?
Most pet parents notice improvements in digestion (like firmer stools) within 3-7 days. Behavioral benefits (like reduced anxiety) typically become noticeable after 10-14 days of consistent daily use. For optimal results, we recommend using The Dog Edit™ daily for at least 30 days as gut balance, calm, and immune support build over time.
Is it safe for all dogs (besides the combo)?
Yes! The Dog Edit™ is formulated using natural ingredients generally considered safe for dogs of all sizes, breeds, and ages (check with your vet for puppies under 12 weeks). Our chews are specifically made without common allergens like chicken, beef, soy, wheat, corn, or dairy. As with any supplement, we recommend consulting your veterinarian if your dog has a pre-existing health condition, is pregnant/nursing, or is on medication.
How many chews should I give my dog?
Give chews daily based on your dog's weight:
  • Up to 25 lbs: 1 Chew Daily
  • 26 - 75 lbs: 2 Chews Daily
  • Over 75 lbs: 3 Chews Daily
Chews can be given as a treat or crumbled over food. Always provide fresh water.

What are Postbiotics? How are they different from Probiotics?
Great question! Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria. Postbiotics are the beneficial compounds (like short-chain fatty acids and enzymes) that are *produced* by probiotics during fermentation. Postbiotics deliver many of the same gut and immune benefits but are not alive, making them more stable (especially in chews) and often easier for sensitive systems to tolerate. We use postbiotics for consistent, reliable gut support.
